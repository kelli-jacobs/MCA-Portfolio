About Me

Hello! I'm Kelli, a passionate photographer turned coder. My journey into the world of coding began with a simple desire: to create my own website without relying on external platforms. This led me to discover Mississippi Coding Academies, where I embarked on an incredible learning adventure.

Initially, the world of coding felt overwhelming, and I doubted my ability to grasp it. However, with determination and the support of free classes and certifications offered by Mississippi Coding Academies, I persevered. Today, I proudly showcase my development skills and the coding languages I've mastered on my very own website.

My curiosity has grown beyond just creating websites. I now find myself intrigued by the inner workings of apps and programs, constantly wondering how their code functions. This newfound inquisitiveness drives me to explore and learn more every day.

Looking ahead, I'm excited to dive into backend languages and expand my knowledge further. My goal is to become a full stack developer, combining my creative photography skills with technical expertise to build innovative and functional digital experiences.

Thank you for visiting my site and joining me on this exciting journey!
